The Yuan Lab at the School of Computing Science, University of Glasgow works at the interface of machine learning and computational biology. We develop novel machine learning and statistical methods to sequence and image data to answer questions in cancer and virus evolution. With the School of Computing Science, we are part of Inference, Dynamics and Interaction Research Group and the Information, Data and Analysis section. Together with Simon Rogers, we form the Glasgow Computational Biology Group.
